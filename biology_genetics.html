How It Works
Understanding inheritance patterns
📖
Law of Segregation
Each parent passes only ONE allele to offspring
💪
Dominant (D)
Expressed even if only one copy is present
🤫
Recessive (d)
Only expressed when both copies are recessive
🔀
Heterozygous (Dd)
Carries recessive but shows dominant trait

Genetics Vocabulary
Important terms to know
🧬
Gene
A segment of DNA that codes for a specific trait
🔤
Allele
Different versions of the same gene (e.g., D or d)
💪
Dominant
Allele that masks the recessive (uppercase: D)
🤫
Recessive
Allele masked by dominant (lowercase: d)
🔗
Homozygous
Two identical alleles (DD or dd)
🔀
Heterozygous
Two different alleles (Dd)
👁️
Phenotype
Physical appearance (what you see)
🧬
Genotype
Genetic makeup (DD, Dd, or dd)
