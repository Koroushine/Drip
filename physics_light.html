Laws of Reflection
Two golden rules of reflection
1️⃣
First Law
Angle of Incidence = Angle of Reflection (∠i = ∠r)
2️⃣
Second Law
Incident ray, reflected ray & normal all lie in the SAME plane
📏
Normal Line
Imaginary line perpendicular to the mirror at point of incidence
🪞
Plane Mirror
Virtual, erect, same size, laterally inverted (left↔right)
🔍
Concave Mirror
Curves inward. Can form real AND virtual images
🪞
Convex Mirror
Curves outward. Always virtual, diminished, wide view
