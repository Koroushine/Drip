🌱 Roots

Roots anchor the plant in the soil and absorb water and minerals. They also store food. Types include tap roots (carrot, radish) and fibrous roots (grass, wheat). Root hairs increase surface area for absorption.

🫘 Seed

The seed contains a tiny plant embryo and stored food (cotyledons). It remains dormant until conditions are right - water, warmth, and oxygen trigger germination.
