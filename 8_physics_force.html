📌
Drawing Pin

🎿
Snow Shoes
Large area = Low pressure = doesn't sink in snow
🐪
Camel Feet
Wide feet spread weight = walk on sand easily
🏗️
Building Foundation
Wide base reduces pressure on ground
🪡
Needle

Key Concepts
Force and Pressure basics
💪
Force
A push or pull that changes or tends to change the state of an object. SI Unit: Newton (N)
📐
Pressure
Force per unit area. P = F/A. SI Unit: Pascal (Pa)
🤝
Contact Force
Force that acts when objects are in physical contact. e.g., Friction, Muscular force
🧲
Non-Contact Force
Force that acts without physical contact. e.g., Gravity, Magnetic, Electrostatic
🌍
Gravitational Force
Attraction between any two masses. Earth pulls everything toward its center
⚡
Electrostatic Force
Force between charged objects. Like charges repel, unlike charges attract
🧲
Magnetic Force
Attraction or repulsion between magnets. Like poles repel, unlike poles attract
💪
Muscular Force
Force generated by muscles. Used in lifting, pushing, pulling, and walking
