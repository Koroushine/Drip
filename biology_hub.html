✦ 6 Visual & Interactive Simulations ✦

Cell Explorer
Class 8, 9, 10
Build a plant or animal cell by dragging organelles into place. Explore the microscopic world and compare cell types.
🧬 Build a Cell 🔬 Microscope View 📊 Plant vs Animal
Human Body Systems
Class 9, 10
Journey through the circulatory, respiratory, and digestive systems. See how blood flows, lungs breathe, and food travels.
🫀 Circulatory Highway 🫁 Breathing Simulator 🍎 Digestive Journey
Genetics & Heredity
Class 10
Build a DNA helix, solve Punnett squares to predict traits, and explore how genetic information is passed down.
🧬 DNA Builder 🧬 Punnett Solver 🖐️ Fingerprint Match
The Invisible World
Class 8
Hunt for microbes! Zoom in from your hand to bacteria on your skin. Learn about friendly and harmful microorganisms.
🦠 Microbe Hunter 🔬 Microscope Zoom ✅ Good vs Bad Sorter
Plant Anatomy & Reproduction
Class 8, 9, 10
Dissect a flower virtually, watch pollination in action, and adjust sunlight/CO₂ to see how plants photosynthesize.
🌱 Flower Dissection 🐝 Pollination Simulator 🍃 Photosynthesis Lab
Ecology & Our Environment
Class 8, 10
Build a food web, explore the oxygen-carbon cycle, and discover how different biomes sustain unique life forms.
🔗 Food Web Builder ♻️ Carbon Cycle 🌍 Biome Explorer
