🦠 Bacteria

Single-celled organisms without a nucleus (prokaryotes). Found everywhere - soil, water, air, and inside our bodies. Some cause diseases like cholera and typhoid, while others help in making curd and decomposing waste.

Useful Microorganisms
How microbes help us every day

✅ In Food

🥛 Lactobacillus - Makes curd/yogurt from milk
🍞 Yeast (Saccharomyces) - Makes bread rise
🧀 Fungi & Bacteria - Cheese production
🥒 Lactobacillus - Pickle fermentation

✅ In Medicine & Environment

💊 Penicillium fungus - Antibiotic penicillin
💉 Bacteria - Making vaccines & insulin
🌱 Rhizobium - Nitrogen fixation in soil
🧹 Decomposer bacteria - Clean environment
Harmful Microorganisms
Diseases caused by microbes

⚠️ Human Diseases

🦠 Bacteria: Cholera, Typhoid, Tuberculosis
🧬 Virus: Common cold, Flu, COVID-19, Polio
🍄 Fungi: Ringworm, Athlete's foot
🔬 Protozoa: Malaria, Amoebic dysentery

⚠️ Other Harms

🌾 Plant diseases: Citrus canker, Rust of wheat
🍞 Food spoilage: Mold on bread, rotting fruit
🦟 Vectors: Mosquito (malaria), Housefly (typhoid)
🥫 Prevention: Pasteurization, vaccination, hygiene
