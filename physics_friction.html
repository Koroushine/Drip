Methods to Control Friction
Complete guide
🛢️
Lubrication
Oil/grease fills gaps = smoother surface = less friction
🔴
Ball Bearings
Rolling replaces sliding = much less friction
✨
Polishing
Smoothing surface reduces interlocking = less friction
📄
Roughening
Making surface rough increases grip = more friction
👟
Treads/Grooves
Patterns on tires/shoes increase friction with ground
💨
Streamlining
Smooth shape reduces air/water friction (drag)
Friction Basics
Key concepts for Class 8
📖
What is Friction?
Force that opposes motion between two surfaces in contact
🔬
Cause
Surface irregularities (bumps) interlock with each other
📐
Depends On
Roughness of surfaces & how hard they are pressed together
🔄
Types
Static > Sliding > Rolling (Rolling friction is smallest)
✅
Advantages
Walking, writing, brakes, lighting matchstick
❌
Disadvantages
Wear & tear, heat, energy loss, noise
